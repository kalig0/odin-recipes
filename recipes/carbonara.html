Carbonara

Humble ingredients—eggs, noodles, cheese, and pork—combine to create glossy, glorious pasta carbonara. It's the no-food-in-the-house dinner of our dreams.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Bacon
  • Parmesan
  • Eggs
  • Black Pepper
  • Extra-Virgin Olive Oil
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Boil water, when it starts to steam add salt
  2. While waiting on water, cut bacon
  3. Whisk eggs, then stir cheese and add pepper
  4. Add oil to a pan, then add bacon and cook until crispy
  5. Cook pasta in boiling water
  6. Add one cup of pasta water into the pan and add the cooked pasta
  7. Cook the pasta then add the eggs
  8. Mix in the bacon, then you're done
